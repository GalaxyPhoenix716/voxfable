# Style Guide for VoxFable

## Colors
- **Primary Accent**: `#6f2bc2` (Vibrant Purple)
- **Secondary/Dark Accent**: `#36165e` (Deep Violet)

## Typography
- **Primary Font Family**: Poppins

## Tone & Design Philosophy
- **Tone**: Joyful, warm, curious.
- **Target Audience**: Children aged 6-10. App UI should feel game-like, visual, and highly responsive with micro-animations.
